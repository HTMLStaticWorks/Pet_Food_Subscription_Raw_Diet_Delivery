How I Formulate & Prepare the Diets

Every pet's metabolism is unique. Feeding a standard bag of kibble treats all animals identically. In contrast, my custom raw food programs analyze breed weight ratios, gut health history, activity rates, and allergen sensitivities.

The BARF Method (Biologically Appropriate Raw Food)

I design recipes using the BARF standard, ensuring your pet absorbs active nutrients in their natural molecular states:

  • 80% Muscle Meat: High-grade protein providing essential amino acids, taurine, and moisture.
  • 10% Raw Bone: Calcium and phosphorus in bioavailable, digestively safe structures.
  • 10% Offal Organs: Liver, kidney, and spleen supplying essential natural vitamin complexes.

Transitioning to Raw: What to Expect

For young dogs, a direct switch (cold turkey) is highly successful. For cats and seniors, I design a 10-day slow transition, introducing raw food alongside their current diet in gradually increasing ratios.

As the stomach acidity drops to optimal levels, the body sheds metabolic toxins. You will notice smaller, dry, and virtually odorless stools as a result of maximum nutrient absorption.
