MEET THE SPECIALIST

I am Dr. Julian Thorne.

I started my practice after seeing my own dog struggle with commercial pet foods. As a certified veterinary nutritionist, I realized that felines and canines require biological raw foods to truly thrive, not ultra-processed kibbles baked with starch fillers.

For the past 8 years, I have formulated species-appropriate raw meals. I personally source clean pasture-raised meats and mix them in proper clinical ratios. I do not run a massive factory; every batch is checked by me in my commercial kitchen to ensure it meets strict FEDIAF nutrition rules.

My Raw Feeding Standards

Three core protocols I follow for every recipe prep and meal portion delivered.

Clinical Ratio Standards

I construct every blend following the 80-10-10 rule (80% muscle meat, 10% bone, 10% organ) to match evolutionary diet targets.

Traceable Sourcing

I source pasture-raised livestock from local sustainable farms. I know exactly where every ingredient originates.

Flash Freezing Preservation

No chemical stabilizers are added. I preserve enzymes, proteins, and minerals by flash-freezing portions immediately after batch mixing.
