THE PROCESS

How I Work with You and Your Pet

A structured clinical process designed to make the transition to species-appropriate raw foods safe, simple, and successful.

01

Pet Intake Analysis

Fill out a detailed questionnaire describing your pet's breed, current weight, age, activity level, and allergen history.

02

Clinical Consultation

We meet online to discuss recipe formulation, raw safety guidelines, transition milestones, and portion control schedules.

03

Weekly Custom Packs

I hand-prepare, flash-freeze, and portion-control the meals into daily vacuum packs, shipped directly to your door.

04

Progress Optimization

Every 4 weeks, we review your pet's energy, stool quality, and weight metrics, tuning recipe formulas as needed.
